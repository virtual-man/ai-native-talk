在讲怎么打造 AI Native 之前，必须先把我们所处的「局」看清楚——否则一切方法论都是空中楼阁。 Before discussing how to build AI-native, we must see the board clearly — otherwise every methodology is a castle in the air.

1.1 · 本质Essence

AI 时代 = 用算力换时间、换效率The AI era = trading compute for time and efficiency

我的观点 · 一 / My View · 1

AI 时代就是用算力换时间、效率。时间和效率是最大的沉默成本。总之就是一个字——「快」。 The AI era is about trading compute for time and efficiency. Time is the biggest hidden cost. In one word — speed.

6 月 → 1 周开发周期量级压缩 · Dev cycle compressed
25% → 95%任务成功率跃迁 · Task success rate
≈ 0优质工具的边际成本 · Marginal cost

过去用「人月」衡量投入，现在要用「算力 × 速度」衡量产出。慢，就是最贵的成本——它不写在财报里，却决定生死。 We used to measure input in "person-months"; now we measure output in "compute × speed." Being slow is the most expensive cost — invisible on the books, decisive for survival.

1.2 · 根本矛盾Core Contradiction

科技公司的「激进」 vs 地铁公司的「保守」A tech firm's aggression vs. a metro company's caution

这不是谁对谁错，而是两种正确的生存策略，被迫在同一张桌子上协作。 It's not about right or wrong — two valid survival strategies forced to collaborate at one table.

科技公司 · 必须激进 / Tech · must be aggressive

不冒险，就会被拍死Don't risk, get crushed

  • 大模型每几个月迭代，不快就出局Models iterate every few months — keep up or exit
  • 必须容忍试错、拥抱不确定性Must tolerate trial-and-error and uncertainty
  • 创新是生存前提，不是加分项Innovation is a prerequisite, not a bonus
VS
地铁公司 · 理应保守 / Metro · should be cautious

改革非一朝一夕Reform takes time

  • 安全与合规是红线，容错极小Safety & compliance are hard limits; tiny error margin
  • 组织庞大，变革需层层共识Huge org; change needs layered consensus
  • 稳健是责任，而非僵化Prudence is responsibility, not rigidity
我的观点 · 二 & 三 / My View · 2 & 3

这两者存在根本矛盾，是很多问题的本质。青岛地铁已经很激进了——但组织庞大，改变非一朝一夕；放到其他地铁，难度可想而知。 This is a fundamental contradiction behind many issues. Qingdao Metro is already aggressive — but it's a huge organization, change is slow; at other metros it would be far harder.

1.3 · 生存逻辑Survival Logic

想活到最后，不能强依赖项目To last, don't depend heavily on projects

当客户天然审慎、变革缓慢，把命押在单一项目交付上，节奏永远被别人卡住。 When the client is inherently cautious and slow to change, betting everything on project delivery means your pace is forever controlled by others.

现状风险 Risk

强项目依赖Heavy project dependence

收入随项目波动，被客户节奏绑架，现金流脆弱。Revenue swings with projects; hostage to client pace; fragile cash flow.

第二曲线 2nd curve

第二收入来源A second revenue stream

产品化、订阅化、能力复用——把单次交付变成可复制资产。Productize, subscribe, reuse — turn one-off delivery into reusable assets.

资本路线 Capital

同步谋划资本Plan a capital path in parallel

用资本对冲项目周期，为激进与试错买时间窗口。Use capital to hedge project cycles and buy time for bold bets.

我的观点 · 二 / My View · 2

面对这样的行业与客户，想活下去，一定不能强依赖项目，而要同步考虑资本路线、增加第二收入来源——否则一定撑不到最后。 Facing this industry and client, to survive we must not depend heavily on projects — we need a capital path and a second revenue stream, or we won't make it to the end.

1.4 · 交付观What We Deliver

我们交付的，是价值 ＋ 一套会自我进化的体系We deliver value — plus a self-evolving system

我的观点 · 六 / My View · 6

给客户交付的成果包含两部分：① 实实在在的价值（成本降低 XX、效率提升 XX）；② 一套可持续演进的体系与工具——即便项目结束，客户也能自我进化，而非一次性成果交付。 What we deliver to clients has two parts: ① tangible value (cost down by XX, efficiency up by XX); ② a continuously evolving system and toolset — so even after the project ends, the client can keep self-evolving, rather than receiving a one-off result.

第一部分 Part 1

实实在在的价值Tangible value

可量化、可验收：成本降低 XX%、效率提升 XX%、人力释放 XX。让价值看得见。Quantifiable and verifiable: −XX% cost, +XX% efficiency, XX headcount freed. Make value visible.

第二部分 Part 2

可持续演进的体系与工具A self-evolving system & toolset

项目结束后客户能自我进化，而非一次性交付。这正是 AI Native 的延伸——把「会成长的能力」交到客户手里。After the project, the client keeps evolving — not a one-off handover. This extends "AI-native": hand clients a capability that grows.

这也直接回应「不强依赖项目」：把交付从一次性成果升级为可复用、可演进的资产，既提升客户黏性，也支撑我们的第二曲线。 This directly answers "don't depend on projects": upgrade delivery from a one-off result to a reusable, evolving asset — boosting client stickiness and feeding our second curve.

1.5 · 悬顶之剑Sword Overhead

大厂正在向上蚕食产品公司的地盘Foundation-model giants are eating product companies' turf

大模型大厂能力持续快速迭代，不断吞噬原本属于产品公司的工作。 Big model providers iterate fast, steadily absorbing work that once belonged to product companies.

  • 今天引以为傲的智能体、工具，几个月后可能被原生能力覆盖Today's prized agents/tools may be covered by native capabilities in months
  • 「做了一个 AI 工具」本身不再是护城河"Built an AI tool" is no longer a moat
  • 护城河转移到：行业 Know-how、数据、客户关系、合规理解The moat shifts to domain know-how, data, client relationships, compliance
⚠️

反共识：AI 也替代不了的Contrarian: what AI can't replace

大厂模型很强，但读不懂企业复杂的利益链与合规风险。这是我们扎根行业的价值锚点。Models are strong but can't grasp a firm's complex interests and compliance risks — our anchor of value.

5% 陷阱：95% 准确率，意味着每 20 次就有 1 次重大失误——在地铁场景不可接受，也是我们存在的理由。The 5% trap: 95% accuracy means 1 major failure every 20 — unacceptable in metro, and our reason to exist.

我的观点 · 四 / My View · 4

大厂能力一直向上蚕食，再过几个月，很多公司引以为傲的产品都会被干掉。我们必须想清楚：哪些会被覆盖，哪些是大厂永远做不到的。 The giants keep encroaching; in months many firms' proud products will be wiped out. We must clarify what will be covered — and what they can never do.

3.0 · 北极星North Star

先立靶心：AI Native 组织长什么样？Set the target: what does an AI-native org look like?

我对 AI Native 组织的理解 / My definition

不是「装了 AI 工具的公司」，而是 AI 像血液一样流经企业每个环节、环环相扣的组织。具体五条： Not "a company with AI tools," but one where AI flows like blood through every part of the organization, all interlinked. Five points:

01 · 决策大脑 Brain

AI 拥有完整企业上下文AI holds full enterprise context

AI 掌握全量上下文，成为管理者的辅助决策大脑，而非孤立问答工具。AI holds the full context and becomes leadership's decision-support brain, not an isolated Q&A tool.

02 · 经验飞轮 Flywheel

经验实时沉淀、能力进化Experience accrues, ability evolves

员工经验在组织内实时沉淀，AI 的决策与执行能力随之持续提升，越用越强。Staff experience accrues in real time; AI's decisions and execution keep improving — stronger with use.

03 · 零信息差 No gaps

不靠人传人传递信息No person-to-person info relay

信息流转不再依赖人沟通，人与人之间不存在信息差，损耗与失真大幅降低。Information no longer relies on human relay; no info asymmetry between people; loss and distortion drop sharply.

04 · AI 为主 AI-first

屏幕上的工作 AI 主、人辅On-screen work: AI leads, humans assist

电脑屏幕上的工作，AI 为主、人为辅；人聚焦判断、授权与例外处理。On-screen, AI leads and humans assist; people focus on judgment, authorization, and exceptions.

05 · 如血液 Like blood

AI 覆盖所有环节、环环相扣AI covers every link, all interlocked

AI 如同人体的血液，覆盖企业运转的所有环节并环环相扣——不是某个部门的工具，而是整个组织的循环系统。这是前四条的总纲。AI is like the body's blood — covering and interlinking every part of operations. Not a department's tool, but the org's circulatory system. The summary of the first four.

这五条是目标状态。接下来的 3.1–3.4，谈的就是从今天到这个状态，第一步该怎么迈——而它们最终都指向：思想与人。 These five are the target state. Sections 3.1–3.4 are about the first step from today toward it — all pointing to: mindset and people.

3.1 · 本质Essence

AI Native 落地，最终是思想问题Going AI-native is ultimately a mindset problem

我的观点 · 五 / My View · 5

AI Native 落地，最后都是思想问题。工具人人能买，组织流程能改，但「默认用 AI 思考、AI 优先」的心智，才是真分水岭。 Going AI-native is ultimately a mindset problem. Anyone can buy tools and reshape process, but the "AI-first, think-with-AI by default" mindset is the real watershed.

底层 Base

思想 / 心智Mindset

AI 优先是默认选项。最难，也最决定成败。AI-first as default. Hardest, and most decisive.

中层 Middle

组织 / 机制Org & mechanisms

授权、容错、快速迭代的制度设计。Designing authorization, tolerance, fast iteration.

表层 Surface

工具 / 产品Tools & products

看得见、最易上手，但壁垒最低。Visible and easy to adopt, but lowest barrier.

3.2 · 路径Path

靠头部效应：20–30% 先行者拉动Via the head effect: 20–30% pioneers pull the rest

我的观点 · 五 / My View · 5

最终落地还是要靠头部效应——让 20–30% 的人先行，由他们拉动整个组织。 Execution ultimately relies on the head effect — let 20–30% go first and pull the whole org along.

  • 不追求一次性全员转型，先点燃「关键少数」Not a one-shot all-hands shift; ignite the critical few
  • 给先行者真实的授权、资源与容错空间Give pioneers real authority, resources, and room to fail
  • 把成果做成可见标杆，形成示范与势能Turn results into visible benchmarks and momentum
🚀

先行者飞轮The pioneer flywheel

选先行者 → 给授权与算力 → 做出快成果 → 树立标杆 → 拉动更多人 → 心智转变 → 组织 AI Native 化。Pick pioneers → grant authority & compute → ship fast wins → set benchmarks → pull more people → shift mindset → org goes AI-native.

关键：先行者不是「最懂技术的人」，而是「最愿意用 AI 重新想问题的人」。Key: pioneers aren't the most technical, but those most willing to rethink problems with AI.
